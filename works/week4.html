Instructions

1) place one dot on the paper.
2) place another dot horizontally across from this first dot.
3) connect the two dots by creating an arc shape: one above, one below. It should look like a lemon when you do this.
4) once this is done, draw a circle in the center of the lemon shape. make sure the top of the circle and the bottom are touching the lemon shape from the inside.
5) once this is done, draw a smaller circle within that circle. Make sure its small like a pea.
6) using your pencil, measure the width of the lemon shape. measure out the distance of this unit of measurement and repeat steps 1-6.
